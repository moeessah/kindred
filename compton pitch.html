What Compton is actually dealing with

The academic work is done.
Career clarity is next.

Compton Unified has 24,000 students across Compton High, Dominguez, Centennial, and Compton Early College. Graduation rates have improved substantially under Dr. Brawley. The college-and-career readiness rate sits at 43 percent against a 2030 target of 64 percent. That is 21 percentage points in six years.

The counselor caseload mirrors California's average: roughly 491 students per counselor, against a recommended 250:1. In practice, a student gets a 20-minute session, and whatever direction they walk in with is what they work with. Most walk in without one.

When a student arrives at a counselor appointment with Kindred already on their phone, the conversation begins at the second minute instead of the twelfth.
